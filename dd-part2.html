Introduction

It is a self created project. This project has been developed from scratch by me as an assignment project for the specialization course "UX design" by California Institute of Arts in coursera.

This is the second of the two parts of the project "The Dosa Diaries" which shows wire frames and prototype designs for the website developed.

Wireframes



Assumption

Assuming that most of the users will be accessing the site from their mobile device, my main focus was to develop wireframes for mobile device layouts. Since the business is mainly targeting the office workers and due to security reasons employees are generally not allowed to access any sites other than office sites from office desktops and systems. Therefore, employees would tend to use their mobile phones to access "The Dosa Diaries" website to order their dosa.
